About the Role
 
We're looking for an experienced Solution Architect to join our dedicated Professional Services team. You will sit at the heart of our mission – empowering businesses to unlock transformative insights from their data. Xemsoft's goal is to make access to data as simple and reliable as electricity.

This role demands the capacity, capability, and drive to successfully deliver projects and solutions throughout the customer journey. This includes securing technical wins in collaboration with sales engineering, creating seamless customer onboarding experiences, providing customer care, maintaining ongoing engagement, fostering consumption growth, and ensuring net revenue retention.

The Solution Architect will act as an internal advocate for customers and bring the voice of the customer into the rest of the organisation to share perspectives leading to an increase in meaningful outcomes and increasing business value to our customers.

Using your experience with Data Replication/Integration, ETL/ELT, cloud data warehousing, and analytics, you will skillfully guide prospects and partners to faster, more reliable, and secure data integration solutions.. You will be directly and enthusiastically supported by a world-class Professional Services team and a great product that “just works.”
 
This is a full-time position based out of Japan. Candidate must be fluent in English and Japanese.
